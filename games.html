Fortress of Lanterns

You're a ghost who inhabits an abandoned fortress. Night is falling, and you don't have an affinity for the dark like other ghosts tend to. Make your way through the fortress and light all the lanterns as fast as you can!

This game is not playable on mobile devices.

Soul Train

Lead your skull pals through the graveyard and out to safety! This was a game jam game made in one weekend for TOJam13 in Toronto. Art was done my myself and Sedona Parnham, Design by Max Manco and development by Matt Carr.

This game is best played with a controller. It's only playable on desktop devices running Windows.

Download on itch.io

Voice Chat Sim

You're a woman playing an online multiplayer first-person shooter with a bunch of random people, and as many women do, you'll have to navigate and deal with the toxicity thrown your way. This was made as a final project for a masters-level critical game theory class.

This game is not playable on mobile devices.
